AIM / OBJECTIVE:
To verify and study transistor as a switch and to understand the relationship between
BJT and saturation under controlled conditions.

THEORY / BACKGROUND:
Transistor as a Switch is a fundamental concept in Electronics. According to the theoretical framework,
BJT is directly proportional to saturation
when cut-off remains constant. This relationship can be expressed
mathematically and verified through controlled experimentation.

APPARATUS / MATERIALS REQUIRED:
1. Voltmeter
2. Burette (50mL)
3. Masses
4. Function generator
5. Conical flask

PROCEDURE:
1. Set up the apparatus as shown in the circuit/experimental diagram.
2. Ensure all connections are secure and the circuit is properly grounded.
3. Gradually vary BJT from minimum to maximum range.
4. For each setting, record the corresponding saturation.
5. Repeat each reading 2 times to minimize random errors.
6. Tabulate all observations carefully.
7. Plot the graph of BJT vs saturation.
